## the basic what-how (brothers) the js is:
JavaScript's object-based nature is the "what" (it uses objects), 
and its prototype-based nature is the "how" (it uses a prototype system for inheritance). 

- The two concepts aren't contradictory; 
- one describes the overall paradigm, and the other describes the underlying mechanism.
